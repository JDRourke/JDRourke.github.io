Jake Rourke - My Biography

I grew up in a small town in Massachusetts. My hobbies for my whole life have
been sports, computers, and video games. My parents introduced me to these things
from a very young age. My favorite sports were football, baseball, skiing, and golf
After graduating high school I went on to attend Bowdoin College. At Bowdoin I
studied computer science and played on the football team.
